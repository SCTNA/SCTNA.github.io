ABOUT US

The SC Trojans Neighborhood Association was created with the goal of protecting rentersâ€™ rights and connecting tenants to vital resources. We believe that everyone should have access to quality, affordable housing and proper treatment from landlords. Our student organization serves both students and non-students living within the University of Southern California (USC) DPS zone. Please contact us if you need assistance with filing a complaint against a landlord or property management company. Complaints against landlords and property management companies can be made anonymously.

OUR MISSION

The SC Trojans Neighborhood Association aims to protect and assist renters within the USC DPS area who have had their rights violated by landlords or property management companies.

USC Housing Clinic:
The USC Housing Law Clinic provides professional services in housing discrimination law, local affordable housing issues, and landlord-tenant dispute resolution. The clinic provides free legal representation to affordable housing tenants within the area surrounding USC through the services of a designated case attorney. Clients are seen by appointment only.
Phone: (213) 743-2165
Email: hlclinic@usc.edu
