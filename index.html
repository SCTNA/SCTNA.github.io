WELCOME TO OUR NEIGHBORHOOD

Our mission

The SC Trojans Neighborhood Association aims to protect and assist renters within the USC DPS area who have had their rights violated by landlords or property management companies.

UPCOMING EVENTS
TNA Day of Canvassing 2.0
Date: 3.24.2018
Location: USC Village, 3301 S. Hoover Street, Los Angeles, CA, 90007

Since its inception in 2016, the USC Housing Law Clinic has provided free referrals, translation services, legal representation, and other renter resources for local residents. Many have faced discrimination, eviction, and landlord disputes, among a host of other housing issues.

In partnership with the Housing Law Clinic, the Trojan Neighborhood Association will be reaching out to tenants around USC campus (within the DPS Response Zone) to provide essential renter resources. Volunteers will be knocking on doors and introducing to tenants some of the basic services that the Clinic offers, as well as handing out brochures about tenant rights.

LAC Environmental Health

The LAC Environmental Health is an enforcement agency operating as part of the County of Los Angeles, Department of Public Health.

The LAC Environmental Health assesses environmental conditions and reduces exposure to health risks; and to educate the public on sources of environmental risk so they are empowered to protect themselves, their families and their communities.

LA Department of Building and Safety

The LA Department of Building and Safety protects the lives and safety of the residents and visitors of the City of Los Angeles and enhance the quality of life, housing, economic prosperity, and job creation citywide. Utilizing timely, cooperative, and transparent processes, the Department Advises, Guides, and Assists Customers to achieve compliance with the Building, Zoning, Plumbing, Mechanical, Electrical, Disabled Access, Energy, and Green codes and local and State law to Build Safe, Well, and Fast

USC Housing Law Clinic

The USC Housing Law Clinic provides professional services in housing discrimination law, local affordable housing issues, and landlord-tenant dispute resolution. The clinic provides free legal representation to affordable housing tenants within the area surrounding USC through the services of a designated case attorney. Clients are seen by appointment only.
